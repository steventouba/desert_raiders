Before You Start

If you feel uncomfortable with WASD keys for movement you can use the arrow keys.

Your goal is to collect all the baubles to advance to the next level. You can also advance levels by collecting the chest located at the end of the level. Be warned though only the baubles will add to your score.

If you value your life avoid the green sludge and the pesky satyrs or you die. Upon death your score will be reset to 0. Hold down the shift or space key to attack.
